List of Diseases

Influenza (Flu)

A contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. Common symptoms include fever, chills, muscle aches, cough, and fatigue.

Covid-19

A viral respiratory illness caused by SARS-CoV-2. Symptoms range from mild fever to severe breathing issues.

Dengue

A mosquito-borne viral infection causing high fever, rash, and muscle pain. Severe cases can lead to dengue hemorrhagic fever.

Malaria

A parasitic infection transmitted by Anopheles mosquitoes. Symptoms include fever, chills, and sweating.

Common Cold

A mild viral infection of the nose and throat. Symptoms include runny nose, sneezing, and sore throat.

Influenza (Flu)

Influenza is a viral infection that attacks the respiratory system â€” your nose, throat, and lungs. It's commonly called the flu. Symptoms include:

  • Fever or chills
  • Muscle aches
  • Cough and sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Fatigue

Prevention: Get vaccinated annually, maintain hygiene, and avoid close contact with sick people.

Treatment: Antiviral drugs, rest, and fluids.

Covid-19

Covid-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Symptoms range from mild to severe and may include:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough and difficulty breathing
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Fatigue
  • Sore throat
  • Headache
  • Shortness of breath

Prevention: Wear masks, practice social distancing, and wash hands frequently.

Treatment: Hospitalization for severe cases, antivirals, and supportive care (rest and fluids).

Dengue

Dengue is a viral infection transmitted by mosquitoes, causing flu-like symptoms and in severe cases, dengue hemorrhagic fever. Symptoms include:

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Rash

Prevention: Avoid mosquito bites, use mosquito nets, and eliminate mosquito breeding sites.

Treatment: No specific antiviral treatment. Fluid replacement, rest, and fever-reducing medications.

Malaria

Malaria is a parasitic disease spread by Anopheles mosquitoes. Symptoms include:

  • Fever and chills
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Anemia

Prevention: Use insect repellent, sleep under mosquito nets, and take antimalarial drugs.

Treatment: Antimalarial drugs such as chloroquine, artemisinin-based combination therapies (ACT).

Common Cold

The common cold is a viral infection that affects the upper respiratory tract. Symptoms include:

  • Runny nose
  • Sneezing
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Mild headache

Prevention: Wash hands frequently, avoid close contact with sick individuals, and maintain good hygiene.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, and over-the-counter medications for symptom relief.
